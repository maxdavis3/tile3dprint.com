HOW IT WORKS

The Tile 3D Print Process

01

Upload Your Design

Submit your pattern as SVG, DXF, or image file. Our studio converts your artwork into a precision tile relief model — or choose from our growing pattern library of 200+ designs.

02

Select Material & Glaze

Choose tile body (porcelain, stoneware, terracotta), relief depth, size, and glaze from our palette of 40+ colors. Reactive and matte options available. Sample tiles dispatched in 5 days for approval.

03

Fired & Shipped

Production tiles kiln-fired to vitrification for permanent durability. Packed individually, shipped nationwide in padded crates. Installation-ready with standard grout joint compatibility.

MATERIALS

Tile Options & Finishes

Real fired ceramic — durable, food-safe, and permanent. Every tile leaves our kiln at full vitrification, not a resin substitute.

  • ● Porcelain body tiles — frost and stain resistant
  • ● Stoneware — natural texture, matte earth tones
  • ● Terracotta — warm handmade aesthetic
  • ● Relief depth 1mm to 15mm — from subtle to sculptural
  • ● 40+ glaze colours including custom Pantone match
  • ● Commercial slip rating available for floor applications
